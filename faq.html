Visitor Guidance

General Rules

  • Respect the visiting hours of the site.
  • Follow the instructions of guides and staff.
  • Keep the place clean and do not throw trash.
  • Avoid loud noise inside historical areas.

Safety Tips

  • Do not touch monuments or ancient objects.
  • Stay away from closed or restricted areas.
  • Watch children carefully during the visit.
  • Wear comfortable shoes while walking.

Photography

  • Photography is allowed in most open areas.
  • Do not use flash near sensitive artifacts.
  • Drones are not allowed without permission.
